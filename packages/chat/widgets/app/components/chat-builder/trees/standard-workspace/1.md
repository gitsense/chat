# Standard Workspace

A versatile, user-friendly structure for organizing all types of conversations. Designed for simplicity and efficiency, this workspace helps you stay organized without requiring complex setup.

## Purpose

- Provide a clean, intuitive organization system for everyday chat use
- Support diverse conversation types (writing, research, planning, learning, creative work)
- Enable efficient reuse of successful prompts and templates
- Help track progress on long-term goals through projects
- Automatically surface valuable insights from your conversations

## Structure

- **conversations/** - Organize your ongoing and completed chats
- **projects/** - Group related conversations by topic or goal
- **templates/** - Save reusable prompts and conversation starters
- **insights/** - Auto-collected highlights, summaries, and key takeaways

## Instructions

- Click the "Options" menu in the top right corner to edit this chat.
- Click the "pencil" icon below to make this page your own.
- Click the "Edit Chats" button in the panel on the right side of your screen. From there, you can rename conversations or move them between folders to keep everything tidy.
